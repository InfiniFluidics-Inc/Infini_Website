Dr. Sagar Yadavali, PhD

Founder & Chief Executive Officer
University of Pennsylvania · AIRFoundry
Dr. Yadavali is the visionary behind InfiniFluidics' patented VLSMI (Very Large Scale Microfluidic Integration) technology. With deep expertise in nano and microparticle manufacturing, he leads the company's mission to 100× accelerate the translation of particle formulations from the research lab to patient-ready therapeutics.
